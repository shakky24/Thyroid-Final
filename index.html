What Are Thyroid Nodules?

Very common. Usually benign. Still worth evaluating.

Thyroid nodules are common, and most are benign and non-cancerous. Even then, they can still create symptoms, pressure, and visible neck swelling that affect comfort and confidence.

They may cause:

  • Neck swelling or visible lump
  • Discomfort or pressure
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Voice changes
  • Cosmetic concerns

The good news is that you no longer need surgery to treat them in many suitable cases.

Treatment Options

Advanced Non Surgical Treatment Options

Your doctor will recommend the most suitable option based on your condition.

Ethanol Ablation

Alcohol Ablation

Best for cystic nodules

A small amount of medical alcohol is injected into the nodule, causing it to shrink gradually.

  • No surgery
  • No stitches
  • No hospital stay
  • Very quick procedure
  • Highly effective for cystic nodules

Microwave Ablation

MWA

Best for solid nodules

A thin needle delivers controlled microwave energy to safely shrink the nodule.

  • No surgical scar
  • High precision treatment
  • Preserves normal thyroid tissue
  • Quick recovery
  • Excellent cosmetic results

Thyroid Artery Embolisation

Advanced option

For larger nodules

The blood supply to the nodule is reduced, causing it to shrink over time.

  • Completely non surgical
  • Effective for large nodules
  • Reduces symptoms
  • Minimally invasive technique

Your Questions Answered

Everything You Need to Know

Is non surgical thyroid nodule treatment safe?

Yes. When performed by an experienced interventional radiologist under ultrasound guidance, these procedures are designed to be precise and safe.

Will I need hospital admission?

Most patients are discharged the same day, without a prolonged hospital stay.

How soon can I return to normal activities?

Recovery is usually quick, and many patients return to routine activities within 1 to 2 days.

Which treatment option is right for me?

The best option depends on whether your nodule is cystic, solid, large, or causing symptoms. Your doctor will guide you after evaluation.

Will my thyroid function be preserved?

One of the major advantages of these treatments is that they aim to preserve healthy thyroid tissue and function.
